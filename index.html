FAQ

Questions We
Get Asked

Everything you need to know before we start building together.

How quickly can you start a new project? +
We can typically onboard within 5–7 business days of signing. Our discovery process is fast — we use a structured intake form and a 90-minute kickoff call to align on goals, tech, and timelines before any work begins.
Do you work with early-stage startups or only established companies? +
Both. We love working with ambitious founders on MVPs as much as we enjoy helping growth-stage companies scale complex infrastructure. Our Starter plan is designed specifically for startups who need senior-level quality at a manageable budget.
Who owns the code and IP after the project? +
You do — 100%. All source code, assets, and intellectual property transfer fully to you upon final payment. We retain no rights, no licences, nothing. You get everything including documentation, CI/CD configs, and deployment scripts.
Can you integrate with our existing engineering team? +
Absolutely. We embed naturally into existing teams — joining your Slack, using your GitHub workflow, attending your standups. We're used to operating as an extension of in-house teams, not as an external vendor who works in isolation.
What's your policy on NDA and confidentiality? +
We sign mutual NDAs before any project discussion. Confidentiality is non-negotiable for us — your business logic, roadmap, and data never leave our secured workflows. We also offer SOC 2-compliant infrastructure options for sensitive workloads.
Do you offer post-launch maintenance and support? +
Yes — all plans include a post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing retainer arrangements for monitoring, bug fixes, feature additions, and performance tuning. Enterprise clients get 24/7 on-call coverage with a guaranteed response SLA.
